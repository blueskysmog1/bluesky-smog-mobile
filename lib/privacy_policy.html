Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Blue Sky Smog ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Blue Sky Smog mobile application (the "App"). This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects information that you enter directly, including:

  • Customer names, company names, phone numbers, and email addresses
  • Customer mailing addresses (street, city, state, ZIP)
  • Vehicle information (VIN, license plate, year, make, model, odometer)
  • Invoice and estimate details (services performed, amounts, payment method)

The App also uses your device camera solely to scan VIN plates and DMV registration paperwork. Photos taken during scanning are processed locally on your device and are not stored or transmitted anywhere.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information entered into the App is used exclusively to:

  • Create and manage customer records for your business
  • Generate invoices and estimates
  • Sync data between your mobile device and the Blue Sky Smog desktop application

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than the core function of the App.

3. Data Storage and Sync

All data is stored locally on your device and, when sync is enabled, on a private server accessible only to your business account. Data is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection. No customer data is shared with any third party.

4. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device camera to allow you to photograph VIN plates and registration documents for text recognition. Camera access is used only when you actively initiate a scan. No images are saved to your photo library or uploaded to any server.

5. Data Retention

Data remains on your device and your private sync server for as long as you use the App. You may delete any customer, vehicle, or invoice record at any time from within the App. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by business professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
